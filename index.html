About Powergentic

Powergentic is an AI-first company dedicated to empowering enterprises with advanced AI tooling and expert guidance. Our mission is to streamline the development of intelligent solutions while ensuring scalable, efficient AI infrastructure management.

From model deployment to lifecycle management, we help organizations unlock the full potential of AI through our innovative platform and enterprise-ready solutions. Whether you're just beginning your AI journey or scaling to meet global demand, Powergentic is your trusted partner in AI innovation.

Powergentic is founded by Chris Pietschmann, Microsoft MVP, HashiCorp Ambassador, Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), and a seasoned technologist and AI advocate. Powergentic blends deep technical expertise with a clear vision for enterprise AI transformation.
